FAQ

Is an appointment required?

Yes, an appointment is required for all detailing services. Make an appointment simply by calling/texting (305) 343.1220 or book online.

What is the “Clay Bar Service?”

When a mold of clay is used hand in hand with our premium soap to extract and remove all contaminations adhered onto exterior paint. (Tar, asphalt, bugs, acid rain, tree sap, bird droppings, etc.)

How does ceramic coating work?

After wash and clay bar we then apply ceramic coating to the entire exterior of the vehicle. Note: 24 HOURS IN AN ENCLOSED SPACE (e.g. garage, storage unit, warehouse) is needed for curing process to be 100% effective.

What is ECO Wash?

ECO Wash is for those who want to take the environmentally friendly route for detailing their vehicle. No water is used for the ECO Wash. We only use the industries best “Ready To Use Waterless Car Wash Spray, ECO-Smart-RU.”

What is your referral program?

Refer 3 new clients to “Eazy Splash Auto Detailer,” get one free Eazy Wash Service.

Do you do stain removal?

Yes. Every interior detail will receive a thorough stain removal to the very best of our abilities.

Can your pressure washer damage the paint?

Absolutely not. Our state-of-the-art pressure washing equipment is always regulated at the optimal PSI to ensure that no harm come to the paint or rims of your vehicle.

What kind of products do we use?
